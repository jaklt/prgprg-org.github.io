We are a group of researchers based in Prague interested in programming languages and systems from the Programming Research Laboratory at the Czech Technical University and the Department of Distributed and Dependable Systems at the Faculty of Mathematics and Physics. We meet regularly, organise joint events and promote programming languages and systems research, projects and courses in Prague.

If you are interested in joining our group, drop us an email or come to any of the events listed below!
